Recipe for Matcha Latte

Here are some steps to creating a delicious matcha latte:

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 Teaspoon matcha green tea powder
  • 2 ounces (1/4 cup) cool filtered water
  • 1/2 teapsoon honey (or maple syrup)
  • 2 ounces (1/4 cup) hot water (175 to 180 degrees Fahrenheit)
  • 4 ounces (1/2 cup) steamed or frothed whole milk (or oat milk for vegan)

Steps:

  1. Add the matcha to a spouted bowl or glass liquid measuring cup. Add a drizzle of the cool water
  2. Combine with water and honey
  3. Steam or froth the milk
  4. Serve
