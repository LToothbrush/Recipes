Recipe for a Lunchables Pizza

Here are some steps to creating a delicious Lunchables pizza:

Ingredients:

  • Lunchables Pepperoni Packet
  • Lunchables Cheese Packet
  • Lunchables Sauce Packet
  • Lunchables Cardboard Crust

Steps:

  1. Spread Lunchables sauce packet over cardboard crust
  2. Sprinkle cheese over sauce-crust
  3. Optional: Put Pepperoni over cheese
  4. Put crust mixture in microwave oven for 1:30 to 2 minutes
